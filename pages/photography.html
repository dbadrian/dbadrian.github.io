Photography

all seasons and their change, all please alike

Besides my passion for hiking and climbing I am into nature photograph and I am happy to share some of that here. Although you might also find some non-nature shots mixed in :).

Some picture carry annotations, which are currently switched on by default. You can switch them off in the top-left corner.

Please mind, you will be redirected to an externally hosted album viewer (backblaze.com cloud hosting) - sadly it is rather slow, please bear with it until I find a better hosting solution.

If you are interested in using any of the images, contact me by mail (see bottom of the page).

Any unauthorized usage is prohibited, even if the pictures do not contain a copyright watermark ;).
