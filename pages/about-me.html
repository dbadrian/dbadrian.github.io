I study Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at the Technical University of Munich. I am interested in robotics, artificial intelligence, as well as theoretical & computational neuroscience. Thoroughly enticed by entrepreneurship, I studied and recently graduated from the add-on Technology Management degree at the CDTM (a joint venture of both Munich universities, TUM and LMU). I have worked (part-time/internship) at university, several bigger companies (Siemens, NTT), and for some start-ups (NavVis, Robotise) - for more details, check my CV.

I love nature, climbing, and traveling and often combine it with my passion for nature photography. Soon this website will also serve its secondary purpose of showcasing some of my photos.

Scientific / Research interests:

  • Generally: AI/ML related topics
  • Computer Vision (in particular SLAM)
  • (Computational) Neuroscience
  • Bio-inspired Computer Vision (Dynamic Vision Sensor!)
