I studied Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at the Technical University of Munich. I am interested in robotics, artificial intelligence, as well as theoretical & computational neuroscience. Thoroughly enticed by entrepreneurship, I also studied and recently graduated from the add-on Technology Management degree at the CDTM (a joint venture of both Munich universities, TUM and LMU). I have worked (part-time/internship) at university, big companies (Siemens, NTT), and start-ups (NavVis, Robotise) - for more details, check my CV.

I love nature, climbing, and traveling and often combine it with my passion for nature photography. Check my photography section if you are curious.

Scientific / Research interests:

  • Generally: AI/ML related topics
  • Computer Vision (in particular SLAM)
  • Bio-inspired Computer Vision (Dynamic Vision Sensor!)
  • (Computational) Neuroscience
