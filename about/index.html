    About Me¶

    I studied Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at the Technical University of Munich. I am interested in robotics, artificial intelligence, as well as theoretical & computational neuroscience. I have previously also graduated from the add-on Technology Management degree at the CDTM (a joint venture of both Munich universities, TUM and LMU). I have in the past at university, big companies (Siemens, NTT), or start-ups (NavVis, Robotise) - for more details, check my CV.

    Formerly a PhD student at the Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence (BCAI), I am now a Research Scientist with Bosch Corporate Research working on the EU Horizon 2020 Project: DARKO.

    Outside the world of tech/science: I love nature, climbing, and traveling and often I combine it with my passion for nature photography.
